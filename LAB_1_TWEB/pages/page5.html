Resident Evil

Full Chronology — 1996 to Present
Home Characters Weapons Games Chronology

From a haunted mansion in the Arklay Mountains to a crumbling Romanian castle — the Resident Evil series has spanned nearly three decades of survival horror.
This is the complete release history: every mainline game, remake, spin-off, and film, in the order they were unleashed upon the world.

1990s — The Origin of the Nightmare
1996

Resident Evil

[Mainline #1]
The Spencer Mansion. The first zombie. The first dog through the window.
Chris Redfield and Jill Valentine investigate S.T.A.R.S. Bravo team's disappearance and discover Umbrella's T-Virus experiments buried beneath the estate.
The game that launched survival horror as a genre.
Platforms: PlayStation · Sega Saturn · PC · Director's Cut (1997)
1998

Resident Evil 2

[Mainline #2]
Raccoon City falls into a sea of the undead. Leon Kennedy's first day on the job and Claire Redfield's search for her brother converge at the Raccoon Police Department.
Two campaigns, one city, Mr. X relentlessly hunting you through the halls.
Perhaps the most beloved entry in the entire series.
Platforms: PlayStation · Nintendo 64 · PC · GameCube (2003)
1999

Resident Evil 3: Nemesis

[Mainline #3]
Set before and during RE2's events. Jill Valentine attempts to escape Raccoon City while a relentless bioweapon — Nemesis — hunts her through the burning streets.
Introduced dynamic encounters, live selection choices, and the most iconic monster the series ever produced.
Platforms: PlayStation · PC · Dreamcast

2000s — Expansion and Revolution
2000

Resident Evil — Code: Veronica

[Mainline #4]
Claire is captured by Umbrella and imprisoned on Rockfort Island.
The first game in the series with real-time 3D backgrounds.
Introduces Alfred and Alexia Ashford, and reunites Claire with Chris in the Antarctic finale.
Platforms: Dreamcast · PS2 (X, 2001) · GameCube · PS3 / Xbox 360
2002

Resident Evil REmake

[Remake]
A ground-up rebuild of the 1996 original for Nintendo GameCube.
New environments, expanded story, Lisa Trevor, Crimson Heads — fans consider it the definitive version of RE1.
Platforms: GameCube · PC · PS3 / Xbox 360 · PS4 / Xbox One (2015)
2002

Resident Evil Zero

[Mainline Prequel]
A prequel set the night before S.T.A.R.S. arrives at the mansion.
Rebecca Chambers and escaped convict Billy Coen fight through a stranded Umbrella training facility train.
Introduced the partner-swap mechanic.
Platforms: GameCube · Wii · PC · PS3 / Xbox 360
2002

Resident Evil (2002) — Film

[Film #1]
The first Anderson film. Alice awakens with amnesia in the Hive, an underground Umbrella facility beneath Raccoon City.
Directed by Paul W.S. Anderson. Starring Milla Jovovich. Earned $102M worldwide.
Theatrical · DVD / Blu-ray
2003

Resident Evil Outbreak

[Spin-off]
Online co-op survival set during the Raccoon City outbreak.
Eight civilian survivors, each with unique abilities, fight through scenarios across the city.
Ahead of its time; still beloved for its atmosphere and tense item management.
Platforms: PlayStation 2 (Online)
2004

Resident Evil: Apocalypse — Film

[Film #2]
Alice escapes Raccoon City as it falls. Introduces Jill Valentine and Carlos Oliveira in live-action.
Nemesis makes his theatrical debut. The city is nuked at the end.
Theatrical · DVD / Blu-ray
2005

Resident Evil 4

[Mainline #5]
Leon Kennedy travels to rural Spain to rescue the President's daughter from Los Illuminados.
Revolutionized the third-person shooter genre with over-the-shoulder aiming and a merchant system.
Arguably the most influential game ever made.
Platforms: GameCube · PS2 · Wii · PC · HD (2011) · Remake (2023)
2007

Resident Evil: Extinction — Film

[Film #3]
A post-apocalyptic world. Alice and a convoy of survivors cross the Nevada desert.
Claire Redfield appears for the first time in the film series.
Theatrical · DVD / Blu-ray
2009

Resident Evil 5

[Mainline #6]
Chris Redfield and B.S.A.A. agent Sheva Alomar investigate bioterrorism in Kijuju, Africa.
First mainline entry with a full co-op campaign.
Culminates in the final confrontation with Albert Wesker inside an active volcano.
Platforms: PS3 · Xbox 360 · PC · PS4 / Xbox One (2016)

2010s — Identity Crisis and Rebirth
2010

Resident Evil: Afterlife — Film

[Film #4]
Alice and a group of survivors seek sanctuary in Arcadia off the Los Angeles coast.
Chris and Claire Redfield appear together for the first time in the film series.
Albert Wesker makes his live-action debut.
Theatrical · DVD / Blu-ray
2012

Resident Evil: Revelations

[Spin-off]
Originally on Nintendo 3DS. Jill Valentine investigates the cruise ship Queen Zenobia, battling mutant Ooze creatures.
A deliberate return to atmosphere and tension after RE5's action focus.
Platforms: 3DS · PS3 / Xbox 360 / PC / Wii U · PS4 / Xbox One
2012

Resident Evil 6

[Mainline #7]
The most ambitious and most divisive entry. Four interwoven campaigns: Leon, Chris, Jake Muller (Wesker's son), and Ada Wong.
Global bioterrorism on an unprecedented scale.
Platforms: PS3 · Xbox 360 · PC · PS4 / Xbox One (2016)
2012

Resident Evil: Retribution — Film

[Film #5]
Alice is captured by Umbrella Prime, a vast underwater testing facility.
Classic game characters return as enemies: Rain, Carlos, One.
Theatrical · DVD / Blu-ray
2015

Resident Evil: Revelations 2

[Spin-off]
Claire Redfield and Barry Burton's daughter Moira are abducted to a mysterious island facility.
Released episodically. Features the series' most psychologically unsettling villain: Natalia Korda.
Platforms: PS3 / PS4 · Xbox 360 / One · PC · PS Vita
2016

Resident Evil: The Final Chapter — Film

[Film #6]
Alice returns to Raccoon City for the final confrontation with the Red Queen and Umbrella.
Reveals the true origin of the T-Virus and Alice's identity.
Grossed $312M worldwide — the highest of the entire film series.
Theatrical · DVD / Blu-ray
2017

Resident Evil 7: Biohazard

[Mainline #8]
A complete reinvention. First-person perspective, Louisiana bayou, the Baker family.
Ethan Winters searches for his missing wife Mia and finds something far worse than he imagined.
Fully playable in PlayStation VR.
Platforms: PS4 · Xbox One · PC · Nintendo Switch (Cloud)
2019

Resident Evil 2 (Remake)

[Remake]
A ground-up reconstruction using the RE Engine.
Mr. X redesigned as a map-wide stalker who cannot be permanently stopped.
Won numerous Game of the Year awards.
Platforms: PS4 · Xbox One · PC · PS5 / Xbox Series

2020s — The Modern Era
2020

Resident Evil 3 (Remake)

[Remake]
Jill Valentine's escape from Raccoon City rebuilt in the RE Engine.
Nemesis is faster, smarter, and can pursue you through previously safe rooms.
Bundled with Resident Evil: Resistance — an asymmetric online mode.
Platforms: PS4 · Xbox One · PC
2021

Resident Evil: Welcome to Raccoon City — Film

[Film Reboot]
A full reboot returning to the source material. Combines RE1 and RE2 storylines.
Starring Kaya Scodelario as Claire and Robbie Amell as Chris.
Theatrical · Streaming
2021

Resident Evil Village

[Mainline #9]
Ethan Winters travels to a snowbound Eastern European village to recover his abducted daughter Rose.
Ruled by Lady Dimitrescu and three other lords — the series' most iconic new character in years.
Platforms: PS4 / PS5 · Xbox One / Series · PC · Nintendo Switch (Cloud)
2022

RE Village: Shadows of Rose

[DLC]
Set 16 years after Village. Rose Winters enters a consciousness-based dimension to remove her powers.
A quiet, melancholy epilogue to the entire Winters family saga.
Platforms: PS4 / PS5 · Xbox One / Series · PC (Winters' Expansion)
2023

Resident Evil 4 (Remake)

[Remake]
Capcom's most ambitious remake. RE4's entire campaign reconstructed with RE Engine fidelity.
Includes Separate Ways DLC with Ada Wong's full perspective.
Topped most 2023 Game of the Year lists.
Platforms: PS4 / PS5 · Xbox Series · PC
2026

Resident Evil Requiem

[Mainline #10]
It is the ninth main Resident Evil game, following Resident Evil Village (2021). It features a new protagonist, FBI analyst Grace Ashcroft, who investigates a series of mysterious deaths involving the survivors of the Raccoon City incident with the help of the federal agent and returning protagonist Leon S. Kennedy.
