☣ S.T.A.R.S. Alpha Team

Special Tactics And Rescue Service — Raccoon City Police Department. Sent in. Never expected to come back. Some didn't.

Chris Redfield

S.T.A.R.S. Alpha team marksman and the boulder-punching legend. A man of few words and an unfeasible number of muscles. Fought Wesker, Umbrella, and his own trauma. Still going.

Jill Valentine

Master of unlocking. Master of survival. Escaped the Spencer Mansion, Raccoon City, and Nemesis — twice. The most dangerous woman in a B.S.A.A. uniform.

☣ Umbrella Corporation

Obedience breeds discipline. Discipline breeds unity. Unity breeds power. The corporation behind every catastrophe. Smiling the whole time.

Albert Wesker

Former S.T.A.R.S. captain. Actual traitor. Gifted eugenicist with sunglasses indoors. Infected himself with a prototype virus for fun. The definitive villain of the series — and he knew it.

William Birkin

Brilliant virologist. Devoted father. Catastrophic last decision. Injected himself with his own G-Virus when Umbrella came to steal his life's work. What emerged in the sewers of Raccoon City was not entirely William.

☣ Survivors & Outcasts

Not agents. Not soldiers. Just people who refused to die. Raccoon City had a habit of making reluctant heroes.

Leon S. Kennedy

First day on the job. City in ruins. Kept a perfect hair parting throughout. Rookie cop turned government agent and philosophical monologue enthusiast. Defeated more bioweapons than most armies.

Claire Redfield

Rode into Raccoon City on a motorbike looking for her brother. Found instead zombies, a mutant Chief of Police, and a child in a lab. Left with Sherry Birkin and zero regrets.
